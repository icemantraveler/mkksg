Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up
D = Down
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
1 = Attack 1
2 = Attack 2
3 = Attack 3
4 = Attack 4
BL = Block
CS = Change Style
TH = Throw
GW = Grab Weapon		 PSP:
1 = ▢
2 = Δ
3 = ✕
4 = O
BL = R
CS = L
TH = (Δ+O)
GW = Select
Pause/Movelist = START
Combo Breaker: (F+R) [3 per match]
Hara-Kiris: Must be inputted by the loser of a match before their opponent can finish inputting a Fatality.

Fatality Distances Explained:
Close: Right next to your opponent.
Sweep: A couple of steps away (close enough to land a sweep).
Far: As far apart as possible (opponent at the other side of the screen).
Anywhere: Hara-Kiris can be performed anywhere on screen after your character is defeated and "Finish Him/Her!" is announced.
