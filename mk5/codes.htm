Master Koin Code:
Choose a vs. game and load a profile for each fighter. Make sure each has a nice amount of each coin.
Wager one round as normal and then after the first fight has ONLY player one open the wager tag but not player 2.
Have player one press / and they will hear a coin sound and then press and you will hear coins being bet.
The sound will stop and hit the wager button again to make the tag disappear.
Now both fighters choose characters and fight with player 1 beating player 2.
Player one will win money but Player 2 WILL NOT lose any. Within a hour you can rack up enough to open all coffins.

Hit BL to open the wager window.

When you open the wager tab with player one the coins are as follows to bet since you cant tell what coin you are betting.
x 1 = Onyx
x 2 = Jade
x 3 = Ruby
x 4 = Gold
x 5 = Platinum
x 6 = Sapphire
Arena Select:
At the fighter selection screen, before you select your fighter, hit SM and a stage selection menu will appear. Hit and on the D-Pad and press 3 to confirm your stage selection. You can then return to the fighter selection screen to select your fighter.

Note: You cannot choose levels you have not unlocked yet.
