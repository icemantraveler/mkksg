Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up
D = Down
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
1 = Attack 1
2 = Attack 2
3 = Attack 3
4 = Attack 4
BL = Block
CS = Change Style
SM = Special Move		 Xbox:
1 = X
2 = Y
3 = A
4 = B
BL = RT
CS = LT
SM = Black
Pause/Movelist = START
Fatality Distances Explained:
In Mortal Kombat: Deadly Alliance, there are no specific Fatality distance requirements.
After defeating your opponent in two rounds, and when the announcer says "Finish Him/Her," you can input the Fatality at any range.
Additionally, pressing the LT button will switch your character into a "Fatality Stance," freezing your movements to make it easier to input the Fatality correctly without any distractions.
