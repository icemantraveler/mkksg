Helper Attacks:
Helpers give the player the opportunity to stun/freeze the opponent for a few seconds.
Their use is limited to two.

Taunts:
Gives you special power depending what character you chose.
Using the taunt is very risky.

Unleashed Attacks:
The Green Unleashed Meter located under the Red Health Meter can be filled by connecting attacks or receiving damage.
Unleashed moves can only be activated when this Green Meter is full.

Slashers:
A type of unleashed move.
This move can decapitate your enemy.
In order to perform this move, you must have full meter, it must be your winning round and enemy's health must be low.
Once you perform a successful Slasher move, Trophy animation will be triggered.

Secret Codes:

1. Zombie Level - In order to play only in this bonus stage, choose your character in the Story Mode and when you see Choosing Your Difficulty menu, type buttons: .
You should notice that Hard Mode changed into Zombie Level.

2. Training Mode with AI - Choose story mode, character and difficulty (any option).
Now when you're figthing, pause the game (by default Esc button).
If your character is facing right directing, hold right button, if he's facing left, hold left button.
If you hold enough time, you should see changed the colour of text, that means you should have got activated training mode.
To turn it off, do the same steps which means pausing the game and holding right/left button.
This also works in Training Mode and Vs. Mode.
