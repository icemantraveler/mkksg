MKKomplete - Terrordrome - Reign of The Legends - Moves List




Official Terrordrome Website
Default Keys:
Movements: B, F, D, U (Keyboard Arrow Keys)
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away/Block

Run/Dash Forward: F, F
Dash Back: B, B
 
Universal:
P = Punch
K = Kick
WW = Weak Weapon
SW = Strong Weapon		 Keyboard:
P = Q
K = A
WW = W
SW = S
Dodge = E/(P+K)
Taunt = D

Grab: D, F, P
Grab Escape: While grabbed by opponent, quickly press Dodge: E/(P+K)
Slasher: D, F, D, F, Taunt

Unleashed Attacks: The Green Unleashed Meter located under the Red Health Meter can be filled by connecting attacks or receiving damage.  Unleashed moves can only be activated when this Green Meter is full.

Helper Attacks: Helpers give the player the opportunity to stun/freeze the opponent for a few seconds. Their use is limited to two.

Slashers: It’s kind of unleashed move. This move can decapitate your enemy. In order to perform this move, you must have full meter, it must be your winning round and enemy’s health must be low.  Once you perform a successful Slasher move, Trophy animation will be triggered.

Taunts: Gives you special power depending what character you chose. Using the taunt is very risky.

Class: Monster
Height: TBA
Origin: TBA
Bio:
Also known as “Tactical Hybrid Ingestion System”, its origin is unknown. a proteiform intelligent organism that can replicate whatever its senses can analyze either by touching or by simple observation. T.H.I.S is in constant survival mode and needs to feed off of other living organisms. After ingestion, it will enter into a state of digestion and hibernation that can last for months before it starts hunting again. It is speculated that it might have come from outer space and is more likely to utilize scary appearances to frighten its prey and freeze them in place.

Info:
Name: Van Helsing (Josh Van Helsing)
Class: Hunter
Height: 1.8 ft
Origin: TBA
Bio:
One of the last descendants of the great Van Helsing family who fought Dracula two centuries ago. He’s the one keeping the “vampire hunter” tradition alive to this day. He inherited most of his cryptid techniques and knowledge from his ancestors and invested some of the family fortune in various businesses to finance his dangerous lifestyle. In time his activities extended to paranormal investigation of all kinds. In recent years he’s also been planning a large scale operation to locate Dracula himself and put a definitive end to his reign, even at the cost of person sacrifices.
