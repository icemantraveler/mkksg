Default Keys:
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away/Block

Run/Dash Forward: F, F
Dash Back: B, B

Universal:

K = Kick
M = Medium Attack
S = Strong Attack
D = Dodge
EX = Modifier		 Keyboard:

EX = C		 XBOX Controller:
P = A
K = X
M = Y
S = B
D = RB
EX = RT

Grab: D, F, P
Grab Escape: While grabbed by opponent, quickly press Dodge: D/(P+K)

Helper Attacks:
Helpers give the player the opportunity to stun/freeze the opponent for a few seconds.
Their use is limited to two.

Combo Breaker:
D acts as a Combo Breaker: when caught in a combo over 4 hits while airborne, press D to recover mid-air and land safely, ready to counterattack.
This costs 2 energy bars.

Unleashed Attacks:
The Green Unleashed Meter, located below the Red Health Meter, fills by landing attacks or taking damage.
Unleashed moves require a full meter.
Each character has a Close and Far range Unleashed Attack.
While Far Range Attacks can be used at close range, Close Range Attacks deal more damage overall.

Death Sentence:
Death Sentence triggers when an Unleashed is performed on an opponent with very low health, executing a final, cinematic attack that finishes them.
