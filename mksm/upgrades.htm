MKKomplete - Mortal Kombat: Shaolin Monks (2005) - Upgrades Choose Your Game

Upgrades
Abilities:

Long Jump: Defeat the Oni Warlord

Fist of Ruin: Defeat Kitana, Mileena, and Jade

Wall Climb: Defeat Reptile

Wall Run: Go back to the burning Wu-Shi academy and break the Statue with the Fist of Ruin and jump down and get the Wall Run ability

Wall Jump: Defeat Baraka (if you don't have the wall run ability after you beat Baraka go through the portal that takes you to the Burning Wu-Shi academy and get the wall run ability) then run up the wall and get the ability

Double Jump: Defeat Goro (go back in after killing Goro to get a secret)

Swing Ability: Defeat Goro, go back to the area you came from, go up the stairs and there will be a portal (go in it) you will find yourself at the Deadpool. Hit the enemies onto the hooks and the ability will appear in front of the door
Health Upgrades:

Health Upgrade #1: In the Wu Shi Academy where Johnny Cage opens the armory so that you can get a sword - right next to the broadsword is Health Upgrade #1

Health Upgrade #2: In the Living Forest after you beat Reptile and get the Wall Climb ability, go to the entrance of Reptile's Lair. To the right, next to the tree you will see some vines and a wall that you can climb up, climb up it and get Health Upgrade #2

Health Upgrade #3: In the Wastelands, go up the wall and cross the bridge and before you go into the next area, go all the way to the left and get Health Upgrade #3 (cliff above and behind you from Wastelands Entrance)

Health Upgrade #4: Beat Kano (Before you go to fight Shang Tsung, Kintaro, and ultimately Shao Kahn, you will notice that Kitana is locked up in a cage, talk to her 3-4 times and a gate will open. Fight the guards and then go through the gate and you will see Jax free Sonya. He will accidentally release Kano and you and Jax will fight him.) Once you beat him Jax will give you Health Upgrade #4 (if you go out and vs. the 3 bosses there will be a health where Sonya used to be. Use it when you're really low on health).
