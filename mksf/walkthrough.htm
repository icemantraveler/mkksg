Level 1 - The Warehouse

Part 1: Warehouse Infiltration

    Start outside the warehouse. Go left and fight two henchmen.

    Continue left, then up the alley. Go right, defeat another henchman, then go right and up to enter the warehouse.

    Go upstairs, beat a henchman using new combos.

    Head right, fight three more. Climb onto the flatbed trailer with HK, grab a small medipak.

    Jump off front, go left, fight henchman, climb ladder with HK, follow grate, grab medium medipak, go back down.

    Go up corridor, then left and down stairs. Fight henchman.

    At corridor end, enter small room on left, grab 9mm clip.

    Go right, between rusted boilers, grab power room key.

    Backtrack upstairs, go right, grab machine gun.

    Exit to stairs, fight 3 henchmen. Enter power room, hit left switch with LP.
    (Checkpoint 1)

Part 2: The Upper Floors

    Return to warehouse, head left to elevator.

    Take elevator to 4th floor, grab 9mm clip, fight henchman.

    Go up, kill armed guard, take 9mm clip.

    Head left, fight 2 henchmen. Go up/right, grab large medipak.

    Bottom-left corner, enter corridor, fight 1 then 3 henchmen.

    Grab detonator, return to elevator.

    Go to 3rd floor, fight henchmen.

    Place detonator on cracked wall, blow it up, grab large medipak.

    Place second detonator in next crack, blow it open, go up/right to grab security key.

    Head far right, use key to open security door.

    Fight 2 henchmen, go right, trigger fire alarm with LP.
    (Checkpoint 2)

Part 3: Office Assault

    Go back to elevator (fight a few henchmen).

    Take it to 2nd floor.

    Fight 2 henchmen, grab 9mm clip, then medium medipak in next room.

    Head down, fight henchman, go right across rug.

    Enter door at bottom, continue right into wooden-floored room.

    Kill armed guard, take 9mm clip, go left, head all the way up.

    Open fire escape door with LP, follow grate up and left.

    Enter left room, fight henchmen, grab medium medipak.

    Go right to boss door, enter with LP.

Boss Fight: No-Face

    Shoot until out of ammo.

    Avoid bombs and flamethrower by circling him.

    Use medipaks and optional detonators.

    When safe, close in and pummel him until defeated.

    Exit right.

Level 1 Complete. (Be sure to save your game!).

Level 2 - The Sewers

Tip: Conserve ammo with short bursts, and always loot defeated enemies.

Part 1: Access Codes & Sniper Rifle

    Head left, defeat the guard, and grab the 9mm clip.

    At first grid intersection, go left, then down. Grab the sniper cartridge on the right.

    Go left, kill another guard, and collect his 9mm clip.

    Keep going left and beat the wrench-wielding guard.

    Go to the top of the screen, grab a medium medipak, and access the first computer to get Code: 3.

    Head down the grid, killing two armed guards along the way to collect ammo.

    Continue left and go down the stairs. Kill the guard and access the second computer to get Code: 12.

    Return near the starting point and enter the door at the top with LP.

    Go up the grate, defeat 2 guards, and grab the sniper rifle.

    Access the third computer to get Code: 9.

    Continue along the grate:

    Grab a small medipak (right).

    Go left, shoot the guard, take ammo.

    Go up and left twice to kill 2 guards, and grab a sniper cartridge and medipak.

    Return to the top and open the door with LP, then jump down the shaft.

Part 2: Key Cards & Bridge Access

    Kill the armed guard, enter the next door, and defeat 3 more guards.

    Head up and left, then down the ramp to grab a sniper cartridge.

    Go up to the center platform and enter Code: 3129 into the computer.

    Exit left, go up the ramp, and enter the newly opened door at the top.

    Inside, grab 2 sniper cartridges, then take the first left.

    Kill 2 guards, go down the grate, beat a guard, and grab a small medipak.

    Go up to the top door, kill 2 guards inside, and proceed forward.

    Kill 2 more guards, then beat another one.

    Go left, enter room, kill guard, and collect ammo + key card.

    Exit and go right to the next door. Enter and grab shotgun shells.

    Backtrack left, down grate, through door, then right and up to another door.

    Kill 3 guards and grab a large medipak.

    Go up the grate, beat guard, then head up and right to another room.

    Kill guard inside, grab ammo + key card.

    Exit, go left, down, and up to next door - enter to start Part 3.

Part 3: Final Key Cards & Boss Bridge Access

    Go up the ramp and enter the room. Kill the guard.

    Go right into the next room, kill another guard, and take the shotgun.

    Head down and far right to a small stairway.

    Enter the door, kill the guard, take ammo + key card.

    Exit, cross the water, go through the top gap and door.

    Kill the guard, then open the left door.

    Go down ladder, kill another guard.

    Head down and right, then up to kill a guard and take his ammo.

    Climb the wall on the left for shotgun shells, drop off, grab more.

    Go back down, open the door on the right, and go up the ladder.

    Follow the grate to the next room, kill guard, and continue up.

    Go right, down, and left to the next door.

    Inside, grab a key card, then backtrack.

    Head up to the topmost door, kill the guard, and grab a medium medipak.

    Go through the door at the bottom for another key card.

    Backtrack: right → down → left → down grate → left → up ladder.

    Return to the grate walkway and access all three computers to rotate the bridges.

    Exit toward the bottom, go right, and enter the top opening to cross the bridge.

Boss Fight: Tasia

    Get in close and melee her.

    When she teleports, follow quickly and continue attacking.

    Repeat until she’s defeated.

Level 2 Complete. (Be sure to save your game!).

Return to Top
Level 3 - The Towers

Tip: Examine all car license plates in the garage by standing at the front and pressing LP - you'll need these codes later.

Part 1: Parking Garage

    Enter garage, beat guard.

    Check every license plate with LP.

    Go left, beat another guard, then head bottom-left, enter room, defeat guard, press red button to open garage door.

    Exit room, head top, through garage door, go left, fight guard, then go up, fight 2 more.

    Use access pad (top-left) → enter 82931 → door opens.

    Inside, fight 2 guards, go left, then up to grab large medipak.

    Head down, then up-right, kill armed guard for ammo.

    Use second access pad → enter 97482 → go through.

    Fight 2 guards, enter toll booth in center, grab Red Key.

    Backtrack to first access pad area → go right, use Red Key to open glass door with LP.

    Enter next door to begin Part 2.

Part 2: Key Hunt & Elevator Activation

    Go up stairs, then right, fight 2 guards, grab ammo.

    Continue up red carpet hallway, kill guard, take ammo.

    Enter left door, grab C Room Key.

    Exit, go right, then down stairs.

    Enter locker room, go right, down hall, kill guard, take ammo.

    Keep going right, then down, then right again.

    Fight guard near door on right, enter, grab BR Key and Free Life.

    Backtrack upstairs to first red door, use BR Key to enter.

    Access computer, then exit room.

    Go right, down stairs, and re-enter locker room.

    Follow red-haired woman to locker, open it for SPF Key.

    Go back upstairs to the first key room, go left, open door, and grab:

    Rocket Launcher

    CR Key

    Kill guard, grab ammo + large medipak

    Exit to red carpet hall, go down, then right, open next door.

    Access computer to activate elevator.

    Exit room, go left, then up red carpet to gold elevator (left side).

Part 3: Rooftop Maze & Sunroofs

    Exit elevator, go right, kill guard, and continue.

    Open gate at top, climb ladder, kill 2 guards.

    Move forward, kill more guards, go right, kill another.

    Drop down opening, kill guard, grab ammo.

    Find detonator, use it on glass sunroof, drop down, grab medipak.

    Repeat with second sunroof, kill guard, grab ammo + Bronze Key.

    Go up ladders, kill guards, collect ammo.

    Drop down next ladder, kill guard (bottom-right), grab detonator.

    Go up-left, grab small medipak, blow both sunroofs, loot both rooms.

    Climb back up main ladder, go left, then up, kill guard.

    At the end, enter door on left, use computer to unlock gate.

    Exit, go right, open door → Boss fight begins.

Boss Fight – Jarek

    Avoid projectiles by circling him.

    Close in, stand your ground, and melee fight.

    Use medipaks as needed to survive the slugfest.

Level 3 Complete. (Be sure to save your game!).

Return to Top
Level 4 - Sinkiang

Part 1: The Cliffs

    Run forward, grab sniper cartridge and rockets, then retreat.

    Use rocket launcher to take out snipers (right, left, bridge, right again).

    Enter the room ahead, collect medipak, sniper cartridge, and rockets.

    Exit, go left, take out more snipers on bridges and sides.

    Grab rockets and medipak, continue through waves of snipers.

    Enter room ahead, collect Blue Key and medipak.

    Go left, grab more sniper cartridges, defeat more snipers.

    Continue forward, killing snipers and collecting items.

    At the bottom, open the door to begin Part 2.

Part 2: The Temple Complex

    Use sniper rifle to headshot 2 guards.

    Blow cracked wall on left with detonator, grab Emerald.

    Blow cracked wall on right, push crates aside to find switch. Flip it.

    Repeat with next crate set and flip second switch.

    Blow another wall on left (you’ll return here later).

    Enter room on left, grab Grenade Launcher + grenades.

    Kill 2 guards ahead, then go into right hall, access computer to lower lift.

    Enter left hall, move crates to reveal Free Life.

    Cross bridge, enter room on left, access computer to raise lift.

    Kill guard, grab Amethyst.

    Go right, down hall, kill guard, grab Blue Key in left room.

    Continue left, cross bridge, grab Ruby, drop through floor opening.

    Kill guard, return to main hall.

    Place gems:

    Emerald → Altar of Queen Eliza (right side)

    Amethyst → Altar of Princess Adrian (bottom-left room)

    Ruby → Altar of Queen Rhiana (top-left)

    After hearing “Excellent!”, cross bridge to MK Dragon Wall.

Part 3: The Inner Sanctuary

    Climb last rock on right, follow ledge to wall opening.

    Inside: go right, up ramp, grab sniper cartridge.

    Kill guard, climb wall, grab rocket + medipak.

    Drop to middle, kill 2 guards, grab shotgun shells.

    Climb opposite wall, grab grenades + Gold Key.

    Drop down, go up ramp, enter room, kill 3 guards.

    Go upstairs, access computer to move plank.

    Enter right room, kill 3 more guards, grab medipaks.

    Go back down, cross plank to portal.

Boss Fight – Tremor

    Avoid projectiles, then get in close and use combos.

    When defeated, walk through the portal.

Level 4 Complete. (Be sure to save your game!).

Return to Top
Level 5 - Outworld

Part 1: The Dragon’s Eyes

    Go right, defeat 2 guards, then head down at the pillars.

    Enter portal on right, then go left, up, left, and down center platform.

    Grab Dragon’s Eye #1.

    Go left, up the left platform, fight guard, grab medipak, and enter portal.

    Go left, defeat 2 guards, grab rockets, then drop from bridge center to land below.

    Enter portal, go forward, then left, down ramp, fight guard.

    Cross crooked bridge, enter portal, flip the switch, and return.

    Repeat drop from bridge center, defeat guard, and grab Dragon’s Eye #2.

    Head back across the bridges to the pillars.

    Go forward, then right, through portal.

    Cross bridge, head down, then left, then up ramp.

    Place both Dragon’s Eyes on altars.

    Enter door with MK Dragon.

Part 2: Lower Platforms

    Drop off platform, grab medipak.

    Go forward, defeat 4 guards, grab sniper cartridge.

    Go left, fight more guards, then drop from center of bridge.

    Go forward, right, forward, then right again, and drop onto portal.

    Walk forward and enter next portal.

Part 3: Tower Climb

    Go right, climb short wall, follow path upward and around tower.

    Kill guard, grab medipak, drop down to fight another.

    Enter tower, exit right, and climb wall to get Bronze Key.

    Re-enter tower, move forward, open bronze door, climb ladder.

    Kill guard, drop through roof hole, defeat another guard.

    Go right, open tower door, grab Blue Key.

    Return to ladder tower, climb up, move right, and drop into next tower.

    Exit, go right, then left and right again. Kill guards, grab medipak.

    Use Blue Key on tower door, climb ladder, kill guard.

    Drop to platform surrounding portal. Enter portal.

Boss Fight – Kano

    Watch cutscene with Jax vs. Kano.

    Use all weapons, dodge projectiles, and stay aggressive.

    Defeat Kano and stand under levitating gem.

Game Complete!
Cheat menu unlocked!
Don’t forget to save your game!
