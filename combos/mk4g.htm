Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
HP = High Punch
LP = Low Punch
HK = High Kick
LK = Low Kick
BL = Block
RN = Run

Side Step In: (Hold D), RN RN
Side Step Out: RN RN		 Dreamcast:
HP = X
LP = A
HK = Y
LK = B
BL = TL
RN = TR

Side Step In: (Hold D), TR TR
Side Step Out: TR TR
Selectable Characters:
Universal Combos:
4-Hit Pop-up Combo: HP, HP, HK, (D+HP)
3-Hit Pop-up Combo: HP, HP, (D+HP)
3-Hit Pop-up Combo: HK, HK, (D+HK)
3-Hit Pop-up Combo: HP, HK, (D+HK)
3-Hit Basic Combo: HK, (B+LP), LP
