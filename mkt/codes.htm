MKT - Nintendo 64 - Codes:

Play as Khameleon:
Press LK, HK, LP, HP, RN, HK, LK during the story screen.
(C →, C  ↑, A, B, C  ↓, C  ↑, C →)
The name "Khameleon" will be spoken to confirm correct code entry.
Khameleon's moves, fatalities, combos depend on what color her name is on her life bar.
If her name is blue, she is Kitana; when it is green she is Jade; and when it is purple she is Mileena.
Unlimited Credits:
Press during the story screen. A sound will confirm correct code entry.
After the next match is lost, "freeplay" will appear in the remaining credits window.
Enable BLUE "?" Menu:
Press RN, HP, LP during the story screen.
Shao Kahn will say "Outstanding" to confirm correct code entry.
Press (+Start) at the options screen to display a second question mark icon.
Enable both RED and BLUE "?" Menus:
Quickly press BL, HK, LK, RN, LP, HP, HP, HP, LP, LP during the story screen.
A sound will confirm correct code entry. The red and blue question mark menus will be accessible.
These question marks will open up some hidden extra options which are outlined below.

Red Question Mark:
Freeplay - ON/OFF
Fatality Time - ON/OFF
Collision Boxes - ON/OFF
One Round Matches - ON/OFF

Blue Question Mark:
Level Select - ON/OFF
Throwing - ENABLED/DISABLED
Unlimited Run - ENABLED/DISABLED
Bloody Kombat - ON/OFF
Smoke - ON/OFF
Khameleon - ON/OFF
Extra Options:
Press (+Start) on the Kombat Mode selection screen.
Options to enable or disable timer, aggressor, blood, and combos will appear.
Random Select:
Player 1: Press (+Start) on Noob Saibot
Player 2: Press (+Start) on Rain
Stage Select:
(+Start) on Sonya
Bonus Pong game:
Fight 50 two player matches consecutively.
Bonus Galaga Game:
Fight 100 two player matches consecutively and a game called Land Of Rellim will begin.
Bonus Space Invaders Game:
Press Z when an object appears over the moon on The Pit stage of a two player match.
The sound of a bell will confirm correct code entry.
The winner of that round will play Invaders From Space.
Fight Khameleon:
Quickly press (+Start) if the word "Toasty" is heard on the Star Bridge stage.
This usually happens after a number of uppercuts are successfully made.
The phrase "Player 1, get ready" will appear followed by the Living Forest stage with Khameleon.

Sometimes Khameleon appears before the match begins and gives clues on how to fight her. Here are all of them:

8. AM THE MASTER OF DISGUISE
9. WAIT FOR TOASTY
Play as Human Smoke:
Choose Cyber-Ninja Smoke as a character.
Hold (B+HP+HK+BL+RN) before the "Fight" screen appears or between rounds.
Cyber-Ninja Smoke will explode and change into Human Smoke.

Sometimes Human Smoke appears before the match begins and gives clues on how to unlock him. Here are all of them:

Play as Motaro:
Select any character to fight in the Jade's Desert, Khan's Tower, or The Wasteland stage.
Hold (B+LK+HK) before the match begins. Motaro will replace your character after an explosion.
You will revert back to your original character after winning the match.
To continue playing as Motaro after the match, enter the code on round one and round two in a two player game.
Then, player two should choose not to continue.
Motaro will be playable in a one player game until a match is lost.

Sometimes Motaro appears before the match begins and gives clues on how to unlock him.
Here are all of them:

1. SEARCH FOR ME IN THE WASTELAND
2. BRING ON GORO
3. BRING ON KINTARO
4. I LIKE JADES DESERT
5. BEFORE ONE FIGHTS IS THE TIME
6. KICKS ARE THE KEY
7. I WILL CRUSH ALL
8. THE TOWER IS A FAVORITE
Play as Shao Kahn:
Select any character to fight in The Rooftop, Pit Three, or Khan's Cave stage.
Hold (+LP+HP) before the match begins. Shao Kahn will replace your character after an explosion.
You will revert back to your original character after winning the match.
To continue playing as Shao Kahn after the match, enter the code on round one and round two in a two player game.
Then, player two should choose not to continue.
Shao Kahn will be playable in a one player game until a match is lost.

Sometimes Shao Kahn appears before the match begins and gives clues on how to unlock him.
Here are all of them:

1. FIND ME ON THE ROOFTOP
2. PUNCHES ?
3. BEFORE FIGHT BUTTONS PUSHED
4. ARE YOU WORTHY?
5. MY KAVE IS A GOOD SPOT
6. DONT FORGET TO DUCK DOWN
6. TRY THE PIT III
7. I CANNOT BE DEFEATED
Super endurance mode:
In a single player game, highlight Kano and press (+Start) on the character select screen.
The screen will shake and Shao Kahn will say "You'll never win" to confirm correct code entry.
Choose your character and select the hardest path.
All matches except the first two and the last will be endurance matches.
The first match will be against Khameleon, the second match will be against Motaro and the last match will be against Shao Kahn.
Some of the endurance matches have at least six opponents to fight in the same match.
Shao Kahn's Lost Treasures:
Select "8-Player Kombat" from the mode select screen and win the contest.
You may select one of the treasures:

Top row (from left to right):
Ending, Galaxian-like game, Fight Ermac, Fight Noob Saibot, Random Prize, Fatality Demo 1, Brutality Demo 2, Fatality Demo 7, Noob + Ermac Endurance round, MK2 Classic Endurance, Mega Endurance, and Old School Kombat.

Bottom row (from left to right):
Past History (Pong), Male Ninja Kombat, Animality Demo 1, Brutality Demo 1, Friendship Demo 1, Fight Khameleon, Fatality Demo 4, Animality Demo 2, Female Ninja Kombat, Supreme Demonstration (every "Finish Him/Her"), Play Invaders From Space, and the Secrets of MKTrilogy.

Only the first five treasures (up to Random Prize) are selectable on the Novice Tower. Only the first eleven (up to Mega Endurance Kombat) are selectable on the Warrior Tower. Only the treasures up to and including Friendship Demo 1 are selectable on the Master Tower or the 8 Player Kombat mode. Only the treasures up to and including Invaders From Space are selectable on the Champion Tower. All treasures are selectable on the Champion Tower with Super Endurance Path mode enabled.
Exploding Babalities:
Perform a Babality move.
Hold (LP+HP+LK+HK) when the screen turns black and your opponent turns into a baby.
Continue to hold the buttons as the baby turns back into an adult and explodes.
Hidden fatality sounds:
Hold LP on both Player 1  and Player 2 controllers to hear Shao Kahn say "Crispy" during the Scorpion's Lair fatality.
Hold BL on both Player 1  and Player 2 controllers to hear Dan Forden say "Crispy" during the same fatality.
Hold during the Dead Pool fatality to hear a gurgling sound.
Easy Ultimate Kombat Codes:
To enter an Ultimate Kombat Kode at any time, simply rotate the control pad or analog control stick a couple of times clockwise at the Story screen or during a demo.
It will go to the Kombat Kode screen as if you had just beat the game.
The purpose of the UKK in Mortal Kombat Trilogy remains unknown.
