Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
HP = High Punch
LP = Low Punch
HK = High Kick
LK = Low Kick
BL = Block
RN = Run		 Nintendo 64:
HP = B
LP = A
HK = C  ↑
LK = C →
BL = C ←/ R
RN = C  ↓/ L
Babalities & Friendships: You must not press BL in the final round.
Mercy: (Hold RN), D, D, D (Release RN) Must be executed at the end of the third round.
Animality: Must perform a (Mercy), then defeating them yet again in the final round of Kombat.
Stage Fatalities available: Pit 1, Subway, Bell Tower, Pit 3, Scorpion's Lair, Kombat Tomb, Deadpool

* = Difference between N64 and PS Versions of MKT

Unlocking Khameleon:
Option 1.) Press "C-Right, C-Up, A, B, C-Down, C-Up, C-Right"
during the story screen. The name "Khameleon" will be spoken to confirm correct code entry.
Khameleon's moves, fatalities, combos depend on what color her name is on her life bar.
If her name is blue, she is Kitana; when it is green she is Jade; and when it is purple she is Mileena.

Option 2.) Enable BLUE "?" Menu:
Press RN, HP, LP during the story screen. Shao Kahn will say "Outstanding"
to confirm correct code entry. Press (U+Start) at the options screen to
display a second question mark icon.
Khameleon can be activated there.
