Grox (Sub-Boss)

Grox is the brutish sub-boss and loyal lieutenant to Exor, sent to soften up the last remaining War God.

A morbidly obese but surprisingly agile being, Grox is not valued for his intelligence but for his immense strength and his willingness to follow orders without question, making him a perfect frontline brawler.

He confronts the final contender before they face Exor, using his raw power to weaken them for his commander's ultimate victory.

Exor (Boss)
Bio:
Exor is a high-ranking soldier from a warmongering alien empire, sent to Earth to retrieve a lost cargo of life-giving Ore.

He knows he cannot defeat all ten super-powered humans who possess the Ore at once. Using advanced alien technology, he manipulates them into a fighting tournament, forcing them to destroy each other for his benefit.

His plan is to let the warriors weaken one another, then confront the exhausted survivor and claim the powerful Ore for his empire.
