Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
= Up/Jump
= Down/Crouch
= Back/High Block
= Forward
= Up+Back
= Up+Forward
= Down+Back/Low Block
= Down+Forward
= White arrows indicate the direction must be tapped
= Dark arrows indicate the direction must be charged or held back for a certain amount of time
= Hold the corresponding buttons, usually before inputting movements with the directional pad/stick

Bio:
Armadon is the god of Life, a spiky, bipedal ceratopsid and the guardian of Urth's delicate ecological balance. For over a million years, he dwelled peacefully in a cave deep beneath the Earth's crust, telepathically linked to the planet's biomass and life force. The meteor strike and the ensuing Cataclysms tore him from his subterranean peace, and the destructive battles for supremacy that followed have tortured the planet itself.

As the last of the great dino-beasts to emerge, Armadon ascends with a singular purpose: to end the war. The conflict among the titans, driven by a lust for control, has caused a nearly irreversible imbalance, threatening the very world they fight to rule. Representing the sacred, Armadon fights not for personal glory, but to stop the immense devastation and restore the harmony of the new Urth.

A noble god and the essence of the animal spirit, Blizzard is the leader of the Virtuous Beasts. For millions of years, he has served as the guardian of natural evolution, and he is enraged by the massive destruction he witnessed upon emerging from his primal slumber.

Frozen for millennia at the heart of an immense glacier within the Himalayas, Blizzard was released when the Great Meteor struck Urth. He lived high in the mountains, descending only when threatened. Now, determined to return Urth to its former state, Blizzard has embarked on a relentless execution mission. He battles to restore balance to the planet, even if it means destroying each and every dino-beast in the process. His immense animal power and age-old wisdom make him almost unstoppable in his quest.

Chaos is the vulgar ape who serves as the god of Decay. In his quest for control and domination over the world, he has become his own worst nightmare. Formerly the mightiest witch doctor on the first continent, Chaos wanted to control the evolution of humanity. During his power quest, he cast a mighty spell that backfired, turning him into a foul, disgusting beast forced to wallow in his own filth for eons.

Right after the Cataclysms, he existed as a renegade scientist who coveted Blizzard's powers and wanted to take control of the new Urth. But his desire to dictate the process of human evolution proved to be his undoing when he conducted a genetic experiment that went horribly wrong, transforming him into a prehistoric ape. During the Cataclysm, Chaos prayed to Throshti, god of Carrion, for release. Now he must do battle with and eliminate the other dino-beasts. When his task is completed, Chaos will regain control of his life and lead his tribe to greatness.

Diablo is the hellish, fire-breathing Tyrannosaurus who serves as the god of Evil and Destruction. As the leader of the Destructive Beasts, Diablo is evil in its purest form, and his followers and foes alike believe that he is evil incarnate. He was trapped in a fiery prison deep within the Earth, to which he was drawn by the pain and torment caused in the great Cataclysms, and was only released when a meteor struck Urth.

Now that he has been freed, Diablo seeks to turn the world into a raging inferno. Determined to pursue his vision for Urth as a dominion of evil, he thrives on inflicting immense pain and agony on his enemies. When he has conquered the planet, he will live just to torture it. If none are left to oppose him, he will burn the entire planet, sparing only the cruel and vicious. The annihilation of all other dino-beasts is essential for Diablo to continue his reign of terror and carry out his gruesome vision for Urth.

Bio:
Talon is the striped dromaeosaur who serves as the god of Survival. For eons, he was the great chief of the Raptor Clan on a hidden South Pacific island. Before the Cataclysms, his home was peaceful and harmonious, but when the Great Meteor rearranged the continents, Talon's island came in contact with the mainland, thrusting him and his followers into an unfamiliar life of power struggles.

Talon is in his most vicious state when it comes to the defense of his raptor clan. Wanting to ensure the safety of his wives and children, he set out to eliminate all dangers to his clan's welfare. On the new Urth, Talon stands ready to take on any dino-beast threatening his people, battling between tribes and other dino-beasts. While dedicated to his clan's protection, Talon also prefers to live in luxury, keeping humans as cattle and hunting them for sport.

Long ago, a sorceress named Vertigo appeared from another dimension. In a great magical battle during the Mesozoic Wars, she was banished to the moon by the arch-mage Balsafas. Vertigo believes the defeat of all other dino-beasts for Urth domination can only be accomplished by utilizing her sorcery and the power of her devout followers... if she doesn't destroy their minds first. Once victorious, she plans to enslave hordes of humans and have them build an insane palace in multiple dimensions.
