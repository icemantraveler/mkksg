Play as (Human) Smoke:
To fight as Human Smoke, select (Cyborg) Smoke and before the round begins,
Press (and hold) (B+HP+HK+BL+RN) until the match starts. (Cyber) Smoke will explode into Human Smoke.
Play as Noob Saibot: (WaveNET UMK3 only)
This only works in 2 Player mode. Select Kano, then before any round begins,
press (and hold) (B+HP+LP+BL+LK) until the match starts. Kano will explode into Noob Saibot.
Crispy!:
After performing a Stage Fatality in Scorpion's Lair,
press and hold both player 1 and player 2 HP buttons to hear Dan Forden say 'Crispy!',
or press and hold both player 1 and player 2 RN buttons to hear Shao Kahn say 'Crispy!'
Frosty!:
When playing as either Sub-Zero, freeze your opponent while their health is in Danger and Dan Forden will pop out to say 'Frosty!'
Stage Fatality:
A Stage Fatality can only be performed on The Pit 3, Scorpion's Lair, Belltower and Subway stages.
Friendship:
A Friendship can only be performed if you do not use BL during the winning round.
Babality:
A Babality can only be performed if you do not use BL during the winning round.
Mercy:
A Mercy can only be performed after winning on the third round of a fight.
After 'FINISH HIM/HER!' appears, press and (Hold RN), tap and (Release RN).
Animality:
Perform a Mercy. Defeat your opponent again to perform an Animality.
