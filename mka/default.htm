Move List/Bios - Game Boy Advance (Universal)

Last updated: 05/12/19
Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
P = Punch
K = Kick
BL = Block
RN = Run		 Game Boy Advance:
P = B
K = A
BL = R
RN = L
Friendships: You must not press BL in the final round in order to set the stage for a Friendship.
Selectable Characters:
Cyrax
Bio:
Cyrax is unit LK-4D4. The 2nd of three prototype cybernetic ninjas built by the Lin Kuei. Like his counterparts, his last programmed command is to find and terminate the rogue ninja, Sub-Zero. Without a soul, Cyrax goes undetected by Shao Kahn and remains a possible threat against his occupation of Earth.
Special Moves:
Green Net: B, B, P
Close Bomb Toss: B, B, K
Far Bomb Toss: F, F, K
Teleport: F, D, BL
Air Throw: BL (In Air, Close)
Finishers:
Fatality - Self Destruct: (Hold BL), D, D, F, U, (Release BL), RN (Anywhere)
Friendship - The Charleston: RN, RN, RN, U (Anywhere)
Ermac

An enigma to all who come into contact with him, Ermac's past remains shrouded in mystery. It's believed that he exists as a life force brought together by the souls of extinguished Outworld warriors. Shao Kahn has managed to take possession of these souls, and use them to fight on the side of tyranny.
