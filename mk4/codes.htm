Hidden Characters:

Meat:
Complete "Group Mode" with all 15 characters, then start a match with any character to play as Meat with the selected character's moves.
Choose Your Destiny:
Press Start to rotate the Arcade Ladder before choosing it, rotating the order of your opponents.
Toasty 3D:
Hold Start during Scorpion's "Toasty!" fatality, and Dan Forden (MK4's Sound Programmer) will say "Toasty! 3D!"
Goro's Lair Fatality Extras:
After performing the Goro's Lair Stage Fatality,
Hold on both joysticks to make the victim slide off the spikes,
or Hold Start to hear Dan Forden's classic "Toasty!"
What Are You Waiting For:
If you wait for an extended amount of time at the "Select Method of Kombat" screen,
the announcer will say "What are you waiting for!?"
Offensive Words:
If you enter an offensive word at the "Greatest Warrior is..." screen, such as "DIK" or "FUK",
those initials will be replaced with MK co-creator Ed Boon's initials, "TRA" or "MAB".

PlayStation:
Hidden Characters:

Meat:
Complete "Group Mode" with all 15 characters, then start a match with any character to play as Meat with the selected character's moves.

Goro:
Beat the game with Shinnok, then at the character select screen,
Hold RN on the HIDDEN icon,
press to highlight Shinnok, and press BL while still holding RN.

Noob Saibot:
Beat the game with Reiko, enter the 012 012 Kombat code in Versus mode.
Exit this match and at the character select screen Hold RN on the HIDDEN icon,
press to highlight Reiko, and press BL while still holding RN.
Secrets Menu:
At the 2 Player Vs. screen, enter Kombat Kode 302 213.
Quit, go to Options, highlight "Versus Screen Enabled,"
and hold (BL+RN) for 10 seconds.

"Endings" allow you to defeat one opponent to see your character's ending.
One Button Fatality:
Activate Secret Options Menu
Turn all Fatalities on
Start a match and anytime during the final round BEFORE he says "Finish Him/Her!", press one of the following button combinations:

(HP+BL) to enable Fatality 1.
(HK+BL) to enable Fatality 2.
(+BL) to enable Stage Fatality.
After doing that, when you hear "Finish Him/Her!", hold (+HP).
Your character will perform the Fatality you chose.
Characters bios:
Go to Kombat Theater, highlight a character, and simply press RN.
