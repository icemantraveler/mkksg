What Are You Waiting For:
If you wait for an extended amount of time at the "Select Method of Kombat" screen, the announcer will say "What are you waiting for!?"
Offensive Words:

Goro:
Successfully complete the game with Shinnok. Enter the character selection screen, choose the HIDDEN icon using RN, and keep holding it, then press to highlight Shinnok's icon, and press BL while still holding RN.

Noob Saibot:
Successfully complete the game with Reiko. Enter the 012 012 Kombat code in versus mode. Exit this match and enter the character selection screen, choose the HIDDEN icon using RN, and keep holding it, then press to highlight Reiko's icon, and press BL while still holding RN.
Secrets Menu:
Go to the 2 Player Vs. Screen. Enter the Kombat Kode: 302 213. Then quit the Game and go to the options menu. Highlight Versus Screen Enabled and Hold (BL+RN) for 10 seconds.
One Button Fatality:
Activate Secret Options Menu
Turn all Fatalities on
Start a match and anytime during the final round BEFORE he says "Finish Him/Her!", press one of the following button combinations:

(HP+BL) to enable Fatality 1
(HK+BL) to enable Fatality 2
(+BL) to enable Stage Fatality After doing that, when you hear "Finish Him/Her!", hold (+HP). Your character will perform the Fatality you chose.
