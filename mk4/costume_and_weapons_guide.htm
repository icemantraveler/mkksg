Costume and Weapons Guide
If you just choose an fighter, you will play as with his/her primary outfit and weapon. Now, if you rotate the icon of the fighter a certain number of times before choosing it you will get a different outfit and/or a different weapon. In order to rotate an icon, hold Start and press any button. Below is a list of all alternate characters outfits and weapons.
