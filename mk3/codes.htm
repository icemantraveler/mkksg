MK3 - Kombat Codes - Arcade:

Kombat Codes are six-digit codes entered at the Versus Screen before a match begins.
Players have a limited time to input their respective parts of the code using Low Punch (LP), Block (BL), and Low Kick (LK).

If both players enter the correct code, a message will appear, notifying them of the effect.
These effects range from health handicaps to unlocking secret battles or characters.
Kombat Codes add an exciting layer of strategy and unpredictability to each match!
