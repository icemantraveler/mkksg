Secret Stage Fatality:
Defeat Shao Kahn to display a message stating that you are the new MK3 champion. Press Start on the controller that was not used to defeat Shao Kahn. The two player selection screen will appear with "-" displayed as the next level. Continue game play to fight on the screen that states you are the next champion. Note: Using an uppercut during this match will result in the fight ending with no winners. Defeat your opponent without using any uppercuts to fight Shao Kahn again.
Always defeated with fatality or brutality:
Lose a single player match and hold on controllers one and two. Your opponent will end the match with a fatality or brutality.
