Easy Endings:
Enable the "Easy Endings" option on the cheat menu.
Then to see a character's ending, start a game in arcade mode and defeat just one character.

Fatal 1:
Enable the "Fatal 1" option on the cheat menu.
Then to finish your opponent, press HP.
This will let any character perform their first fatality.

Fatal 2:
Enable the "Fatal 2" option on the cheat menu.
Then to finish your opponent, press LP.
This will let any character perform their second fatality.

Pit Fatal:
Enable the "Pit Fatal" option on the cheat menu.
Then to finish your opponent, (Hold +HP).
This will let any character perform their stage fatality.

Danger:
Enable the "Danger" option on the cheat menu.
Then, both characters health bars will be very low, enabling one-hit deaths.

Play as Meat:
Start a 1v1 or 2v2 game with both players in Group Mode.
When one player wins 20 consecutive matches, Meat replaces that player's character, using that character's moves.
Reptile Glitch:
In a 2-player battle, have Reptile use Acid Spit while the other player jumps over his head.
If timed correctly, the acid will shoot from the back of his head. Can also be done solo, but is harder.
Switch Your Opponent Order In Arcade Mode:
Press Start to rotate the Arcade Ladder before choosing it, rotating the order of your opponents.
View Character's Bios:
In "Kombat Theater," highlight a character's whose bio you want to view and press (BL+RN).
Highlight the opening sequence to see Goro's bio.

MKGold - Kombat Codes - Sega Dreamcast:

Kombat Kodes are six-digit codes entered at the Versus Screen before a match begins.
Players have a limited time to input their respective parts of the code using Low Punch (LP), Block (BL), and Low Kick (LK).

If both players enter the correct code, a message will appear, notifying them of the effect.
These effects range from health handicaps to unlocking secret battles or characters.
Kombat Kodes add an exciting layer of strategy and unpredictability to each match!
