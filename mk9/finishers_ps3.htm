Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.

Super Meter:

= Enhanced Special Move (Special Move+R2)
        [Costs 1 Bar of your Super Meter]
= Kombo Breaker (F+R2)
        [Costs 2 Bars of your Super Meter]
= X-Ray Attack (L2+R2)/(✕+O+R2)
        [Costs 3 Bars of your Super Meter]
Babalities: You must not press R2 in the final round.

Finisher Distances Explained:
Close: Right next to your opponent.
Sweep: A couple of steps away (close enough to land a sweep).
Jump: One jump distance away from your opponent.
Fullscreen: As far apart as possible (opponent at the other side of the screen).
Anywhere: Distance doesn't matter, can be any range (Sweep or further works best).
Varies: For some reason, the Stage Fatalities in this game have their distance listed as "Varies". However, regardless of the label, all Stage Fatalities should be performed at Close range.

Note: When (Hold R2) is shown in italics for Fatality inputs, it means that holding R2 is optional.
The Fatality will still work without holding R2, though it may be easier if you do.
Just remember to release R2 before pressing the final button in the Finisher combo.
