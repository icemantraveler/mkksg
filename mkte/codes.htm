Perfect Bonus:
While playing 'Test Your Might', break the object when the meter is at the very top and you'll receive an extra 1,000 red koins.
Unlocking Characters:

Unlocking Hsu Hao:
Beat Arcade mode at Normal difficulty.

Unlocking Drahmin:
Beat Tag Team mode at Normal difficulty.

Unlocking Reptile:
Beat Arcade mode at Normal difficulty with every character except Sektor, Reptile and Noob Saibot.

Unlocking Sektor:
Beat Tag Team mode at Hard difficulty without using continues.
OR Link up both Tournament Edition and Deadly Alliance on separate GBA's.

Unlocking Noob Saibot:
Beat Arcade mode and Tag Team mode with every character except Noob Saibot at Normal difficulty.
Then beat Arcade mode on Hard using Reaper Scorpion (Scorpion's all-black alternate costume).

Change Weapon Colors:
The original weapon color is white. In order to change the color, simply hold down a button or a combination of buttons at the screen where there's a picture of the opponent and the stage. Release the button(s) when the match begins.

Hold = Weapon Color is BLUE
Hold L = Weapon Color is YELLOW
Hold R = Weapon Color is RED
Hold (+L) = Weapon Color is GREEN
Hold (+R) = Weapon Color is PURPLE
Hold (L+R) = Weapon Color is ORANGE
Hold (+L+R) = Weapon Color is BLACK
The Final Secret:
Unlock all secrets (reach 100% in your profile) and koffin HG will change its price from ??? to 5000 Red Koins. The koffin contains "The Final Secret". After you unlock it all the characters in the kredits will be in Anime style.
