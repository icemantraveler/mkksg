Goro (Sub-Boss/DLC)
Bio:
Prince Goro leads the Shokan, a four-armed race of half-dragons subjugated ages ago by Shao Kahn. Goro was Mortal Kombat champion nine tournaments in a row. He was defeated by Liu Kang in the tenth, ending Outworld's hopes of assimilating Earthrealm. The Shokan served the Outworld throne until Shao Kahn's death. They refused to takes sides in the resulting civil war and were thus outcast. Few believe, however, that they have seen the last of Goro.
