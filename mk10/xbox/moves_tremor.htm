Tremor (DLC)
Bio:
Tremor has the powers of an Earth elemental: He can create earthquakes, form stone and lava projectiles and shape the ground to suit him. His current status with Kano's Black Dragon clan of arms dealers is unknown, as a recent mission seems to have created much animosity in Tremor towards both Special Forces and Kano.
