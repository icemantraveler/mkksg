Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away		 Dash = F, F
Back Dash = B, B
Uppercut = (D+Δ)
Sweep = (B+O)
Run = (Dash+R2)
Pause/Movelist = START
Universal:
FP = Front Punch
BP = Back Punch
FK = Front Kick
BK = Back Kick
*TH = Throw (Away)
*(F+TH) = Throw (Towards)
SI = Stage Interact
FS = Flip Stance
BL = Block

TH (Away) Reversal = FP/FK
TH (Towards) Reversal = BP/BK		 PlayStation 4:
FP = ▢
BP = Δ
FK = ✕
BK = O
*TH = L1
*(F+TH) = (F+L1)
SI = R1
FS = L2
BL = R2

TH (Away) Reversal = ▢/✕
TH (Towards) Reversal = Δ/O

    *Throw (Away) Switches the character's facing side, while (Towards) maintains it.
Super Meter:

= Enhanced Special Move (Special Move+R2)
    [Costs 1 Bar of your Super Meter]
= Kombo Breaker (F+R2)
    [Costs 2 Bars of your Super Meter]
= X-Ray Attack (L2+R2)/(✕+O+R2)
    [Costs 3 Bars of your Super Meter]
Stage Fatalities: The Kove, Refugee Kamp, The Pit
Finisher Distances Explained:
Close: Right next to your opponent.
Sweep: A couple of steps away (close enough to land a sweep).
Mid/Jump: One jump distance away from your opponent.
Far/Fullscreen: Two to three jump distances away from your opponent.
