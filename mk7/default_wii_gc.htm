Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up
D = Down
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
1 = Attack 1
2 = Attack 2
3 = Attack 3
4 = Attack 4
BL = Block/Ground Stall
CS = Change Style
TH = Throw
GW = Grab Weapon		 Wii (GameCube Controller):
1 = B
2 = Y
3 = A
4 = X
BL = R
CS = L
TH = Z
GW = C-Stick
Pause/Movelist = START
Kombat Parry:
Press (B+R) to parry an opponent's attack.
Perfect timing can be the difference between victory and defeat.
Air Kombat:
Use character-specific pop-up moves to perform Air Kombat combos against your opponent.
Ground Kombat:
After being knocked down, (Hold R) to stay on the ground.
Use the d-pad to roll away or press the attack buttons for a quick ground attack.
Breaker System:
You start each match with three Combo Breakers.
Use them wisely, as they won't replenish between rounds.
Press (F+R) to break a combo, and each use will deplete one icon.
Kreate-A-Fatality:
After defeating an opponent, the "Finish Him/Her" prompt appears.
Perform the Fatality sequence before the red bar drains.
Successfully completing a sequence resets the timer, allowing up to 10 attempts to fully finish your opponent.
(F+R) = Breaker
(B+R) = Parry
(F+R) = Lie Down
