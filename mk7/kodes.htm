Konquest Relic Items:
Collect these items in Konquest mode to unlock hidden secrets:

10 Relics - Unlocks Meat
20 Relics - Gives you 20 000 koins
30 Relics - Unlocks Daegon
40 Relics - Unlocks Pyramid of Argus (Arena)
50 Relics - Unlocks Blaze
60 Relics - Unlocks the whole Krypt
