Ashrah
Ashrah, once a demon hunter, transcended corruption with a holy kriss, chosen by celestial beings to purify the realms. Her mission: to consume darkness and achieve angelic ascension.
In Vaeternus, she battles vampires to enhance her power. Defeating Nitara, a powerful vampire, is now crucial to her quest for ultimate purification and transformation.
Baraka
Baraka, a fierce Tarkatan warrior, served Shao Kahn with ruthless loyalty before seeking new purpose. He aligned with Quan Chi, then Onaga, rallying his horde to seize control.
In the Battle of Armageddon, Baraka led the Forces of Darkness with unyielding resolve, his blades relentless in their quest for domination and the triumph of his new master.
Bo' Rai Cho
Bo' Rai Cho, master of Drunken Boxing, trained heroes like Liu Kang and Kung Lao to combat darkness. His mission intensified after Liu Kang's death, guiding Earthrealm's champions.
As Blaze's rise threatens all that exists, Bo' Rai Cho fights with the Forces of Light in the Battle of Armageddon, drawing strength from his past to ensure victory and protect all realms.
Chameleon
Chameleon, one of the last surviving Saurians, lurks in shadows after Shao Kahns destruction of Zaterra. He hides among Kahn's forces, seeking revenge and justice.
Observing from within, Chameleon plans to strike when the time is right. As Armageddon nears, he aims to thwart Kahns quest for power and avenge his fallen kin.
Cyrax
Cyrax, returning to Earthrealm amid Armageddon, searches tirelessly for his missing comrades Sonya, Jax, and Kenshi. His journey through chaos rekindles memories of his human past.

Dairou
Dairou, a former Seidan Guardsman who fell from grace after executing his family's killer, became a mercenary after his release.
Now neutral and hunting his former comrade Hotaru, Dairou is drawn into Armageddon's chaos with the Forces of Darkness, seeking survival and purpose.
Darrius
Darrius, once a Seidan soldier, became a leader of the resistance after rejecting Orderrealms oppressive regime. His tactics are ruthless, aiming to dismantle the Senates control.
Aligning with the Forces of Darkness amidst Armageddons chaos, Darrius seeks to overthrow the old order and rebuild from the destruction, his legacy shaped by this final battle.
Drahmin
Drahmin, tormented by the Netherrealm, once allied with Moloch in pursuit of revenge against Quan Chi and Shang Tsung. Betrayal led to endless cycles of vengeance.
In Armageddon, Drahmin joins the Forces of Darkness, channeling his rage into the chaos of the final battle, seeking to revel in destruction and retribution.
Ermac
Ermac, a fusion of souls once bound to Shao Kahn, sought redemption after his fall. Freed by Kenshi, they allied with Liu Kang to atone for past sins.
Now fighting with the Forces of Light during Armageddon, Ermac strives for peace and redemption. Their battles are not for dominance but to reshape their destiny and honor their chance for transformation.
Frost
Frost, resurrected from her icy prison, seeks vengeance against Sub-Zero and Sonya Blade, whom she blames for her past betrayals and failures. Her rage fuels her quest for power.
Aligned with dark forces, Frost aims to reclaim her lost glory and exact revenge on those who wronged her. Her path is marked by a drive for supremacy and ultimate dominance.
Fujin
Fujin, troubled by Raiden's dark turn and Liu Kangs reanimation, joins Kung Lao to confront them if they cannot be saved. He investigates these changes and their causes.
Reuniting with Kung Lao and others, Fujin learns of Taven and Daegon's crucial role in a new world order. He seeks to intercept them to uncover and thwart their true purpose.
Goro
Goro, once a Grand Champion and loyal enforcer of Shao Kahn, faced defeat and betrayal but was revived to aid in reclaiming Outworld and restoring the Shokan.
As Armageddon looms, Goro stands by Shao Kahn, fighting fiercely to secure Outworlds dominance. His renewed loyalty and strategic prowess aim for ultimate victory.
Havik
Havik, a harbinger of chaos, subverted order by manipulating events and allies like Shujinko and Kabal. He opposed Onagas unification plan to preserve eternal discord.
In the Battle of Armageddon, Havik reveled in the upheaval, using chaos to his advantage. His commitment ensures that, even as realms face destruction, the struggle and disorder will persist.
Hotaru
Hotaru, once a guardian of Orderrealm, found his quest for balance tested by shifting alliances and conflicts. His mission to restore order led him to align with Onaga.
As Armageddon unfolds, Hotaru's dedication to Onaga persists despite evolving views on justice and order. Hunted by Dairou and facing the chaos of battle, he seeks a deeper understanding amid the turmoil.
Hsu Hao
Hsu Hao, a double agent for the Red Dragon, sabotaged enemies and was resurrected by Shang Tsung after his death. He now serves as a vengeful force of darkness.
Reborn and driven by vengeance, Hsu Hao's renewed loyalty to Shang Tsung propels him through the chaos of Armageddon, aiming to reshape the battlefield with relentless ambition.
Jade
In the Armageddon chaos, Jade's loyalty to Edenia drives her mission to defeat Blaze and thwart the Forces of Darkness, who also seek his power.
Targeting Tanya for her betrayal, Jade fights alongside the Forces of Light, determined to stop her and secure Blaze's power to save Edenia and all realms from destruction.
Jarek
Jarek, betrayed and nearly killed by Jax, survived in hiding, honing deadly new techniques for revenge. His rage led him to Quan Chi, who promised power in exchange for loyalty.
Now fighting with the Forces of Darkness, Jarek seeks to perfect his fatality skills and exact vengeance on all who wronged him. His quest for revenge is fueled by newfound power and relentless anger.
Jax
Jax, scarred by Sektors failed cyborg conversion, returned to the Special Forces with renewed purpose. His cybernetic arms symbolize both strength and the looming threat of darkness.
In the chaos of Armageddon, Jax leads the Special Forces in the ultimate fight to protect Earthrealm. Every battle is a testament to his resolve, driven by the desire to honor fallen comrades and secure a future victory.
Johnny Cage
Johnny Cage, once a key ally of Raiden and Liu Kang, faces a fractured team and a darker Raiden. With Liu Kang fallen and Raiden ruthless, Johnnys visions reveal Shinnoks return.
Tracking Shinnok to Shang Tsungs island, Johnny discovers Shinnoks alliance with Quan Chi. After a fierce battle, Johnny vows to unite his allies and stop Shinnok to protect Earthrealm.
Kabal
Once a Black Dragon enforcer, Kabal was scarred by Shao Kahns invasion and betrayed by Mavado. Rescued by Havik, he rebuilt the Black Dragon to fight the Dragon King.
As Armageddon nears, Kabal leads the reborn Black Dragon, striving to prove their might. Facing foes like Taven amid the chaos, he fights to secure the clans legacy and survive the impending cataclysm.
Kai
Kai transitioned from the White Lotus Society to the Forces of Light, driven by enlightenment and duty to Earthrealm. Armed with Raidens Lightning Staff, he fights with resolve during Armageddon.
Amid the chaos, Kais strikes reflect his mastery and inner peace. His journey from seeker to realm defender underscores his commitment to balance and survival in these final battles.
Kano
Kano, ever the survivor, thrives amid Armageddon's chaos. From leading the Black Dragon to escaping the Red Dragons experiments, hes thrived on deception and survival.
Aligning with the Forces of Darkness, Kano aims to emerge victorious. His goal remains unchanged: outsmart, outfight, and outlast every rival to secure his place at the top.
Kenshi
Kenshi left the Special Forces to hunt criminals alone, guided by his ancestors' sword. His blindness became an asset as he dismantled underworld threats and uncovered a Red Dragon plot.
Drawn by his swords call, Kenshi joins the Forces of Light, transforming from a lone warrior into a leader. He prepares to face the epic battles ahead.
Kintaro
Kintaro, the fierce Shokan warrior of the Tigrar lineage, served Shao Kahn loyally, leading battles and dominating in Mortal Kombat tournaments. His dedication was unwavering.
During Armageddon, Kintaro joined Shinnok's Spire, discovering it was a test for Taven. Now hidden in Shao Kahn's fortress under Mileena's command, he readies for the final confrontation, fighting for darkness.
Kira
Kira, a master of deception and chaos, began her journey in Afghanistan as a covert arms dealer. Her identity exposed, she joined Kabals Black Dragon to reshape the world through ruthless ambition.
Rising within the Black Dragon, Kira embraced chaos and destruction. As Armageddon approaches, she aligns with the Forces of Darkness, determined to bring about a new era of darkness alongside Kabal.
Kitana
Kitana, adopted by Shao Kahn after her parents' death, feigned loyalty while secretly plotting his fall. Her true allegiance aided Earthrealm's heroes against the Deadly Alliance.
Freed from Onaga with Liu Kangs spirit and Ermacs help, Kitana now battles Blaze, hoping to end the endless wars and secure peace across all realms.
Kobra
Kobra, once a violent street fighter in NYC, was saved from arrest by Kabal and Kira, who recruited him into the Black Dragon clan. Embracing his new path, he thrived on the chaos and destruction it offered.
Under Haviks influence, Kobra fought fiercely in Outworld and later, in Armageddon, guarding a crucial bridge against Taven. With each battle, his bloodlust found purpose in the final, world-altering conflict.
Kung Lao
Kung Lao, grappling with Armageddons chaos, faced shattered ideals as Raiden turned dark and Liu Kang became vengeful. His Shaolin values led him to ally with Fujin to restore balance.
Amid the conflict, Kung Lao's dedication to justice drove him to challenge former allies. His journey, marked by sacrifice, sought to honor his Shaolin legacy and bring peace amidst destruction.
Li Mei
Li Mei, once enslaved to build a dark sorcerers palace, was saved by Master Bo' Rai Cho. Transformed by her ordeal, she now fights with the Forces of Light.
Facing the Battle of Armageddon, Li Mei uses her newfound strength to battle tyranny and oppression. Her fight is a beacon of hope, driven by the desire for freedom and a brighter future.
Liu Kang
Liu Kang, once Earthrealms hero, now rises as a vengeful reanimated corpse after being killed by Shang Tsung and Quan Chis dark magic. Driven by darkness, he fights to reclaim balance.
With Nightwolfs aid, Liu Kang battles his monstrous form and seeks redemption. His quest is to restore his true self, confront Shang Tsung, and end his dark legacy to restore harmony.
Mavado
As the Red Dragon leader, Mavado's quest for vengeance against the Black Dragon led him through chaos and betrayal. Despite his skill, Taven defeated him, but Mavado's escape showed his survival instinct.
In the final battle of Armageddon, Mavado aligns with the Forces of Darkness to achieve his clan's goal of supremacy. His legacy of ambition and bloodshed faces its ultimate test as the conflict reaches its peak.
Mileena
Mileena, posing as Kitana, initially seized Outworld through manipulation and deceit. Her plans crumbled when Shao Kahn's return shattered her ambitions.
Now serving Shao Kahn, she captured Shujinko to forge an alliance with Onaga. Despite her current allegiance, Mileena aims to reclaim Outworld and conquer new realms, including Edenia.
Mokap
Mokap, a Chicago martial arts instructor, was recruited by Johnny Cage for motion capture and thrust into Mortal Kombats chaos. Now, he fights alongside the Forces of Light with newfound powers.
Once leading a quiet life, Mokaps world shifted from teaching to epic battles. Despite his heroic role, he yearns for a return to normalcy and his peaceful life in Chicago.
Moloch
Moloch thrives in the chaos of Armageddon, reveling in destruction and violence. Released by Shang Tsung, he and Drahmin unleash torment on the battlefield with unrestrained cruelty.

Motaro
Motaro, cursed by Sheeva's betrayal, leads the Centaurians with rage in Armageddon. Once a proud warrior, he now seeks revenge against the Shokan for their cruelty.
In the final battle, Motaro aims to avenge his people by defeating the ShokanGoro, Kintaro, and Sheeva. His fury turns the chaos of Armageddon into a quest for retribution and restored honor.
Nightwolf
Nightwolf, freed from the Netherrealm after imprisoning Onagas soul, learned of an impending battle and hidden evil manipulating warriors. He joined Johnny Cage against Shinnoks returning Forces of Darkness.

Noob
Noob Saibot, formerly Bi-Han of the Lin Kuei, turned into a vengeful dark force after betrayal. Transformed in the Netherrealm, he commands cyber-demons alongside Smoke.
Driven by vengeance, he clashed with Sub-Zero and manipulated allies like Sareena and Quan Chi. As Armageddon nears, Noob Saibot remains a relentless agent of darkness, pursuing ultimate power.
Onaga - The Dragon King
Onaga, once a near-Emperor of Outworld, was thwarted by Shujinko's power and trapped in the Netherrealm. Freed by Shinnok, he must now deceive Quan Chi to prevent Shao Kahn from reaching Blaze.
In exchange for his servitude, Onaga plans to reclaim Outworld and exact revenge on Shinnok. He aims to defeat Blaze himself and seize the ultimate power, betraying all who stand in his way.
Quan Chi
Quan Chi, master of dark sorcery from the Netherrealm, manipulates and uses necromancy to dominate realms. His alliances with Shinnok shift the balance of power.

Raiden

Rain

Rivaling his brothers, Taven and Daegon, he aims to eliminate them and seize control, with Quan Chis aid against the Forces of Light, securing his ascension.
Reiko

In the chaos of Armageddon, Reiko plans to overthrow Shao Kahn and seize power. His time in the shadows has prepared him to rule Outworld, marking a new era.

Transformed into a vessel for Onaga, Reptiles fate intertwined with battles and betrayals. Even in defeat and transformation, he remains driven by vengeance and the hope to restore his race.

Scorpion

Battling Taven and the Special Forces, Sektors quest for power led him to embrace malevolence. Now a leader of darkness, he seeks to reshape the world with his Tekunin army.
Shang Tsung

Shao Kahn
Shao Kahn, the indomitable Emperor of Outworld, rose from a fierce warrior to supreme ruler through strength and cunning. He united fractured realms, crushing all opposition to forge a vast empire.
Allied with dark forces and resurrected by dark magic, Kahn's rule faces constant challenges. Undeterred, he seeks ultimate dominion, combating rival factions and threats to secure his eternal reign.
Sheeva
Sheeva, a Shokan warrior, once served Queen Sindel and Shao Kahn loyally until betrayal led to her death. Resurrected by her kin, she faced new challenges and found purpose in mocking her enemies.
Amid Armageddon's chaos, Sheevas allegiance to darkness fuels her quest for revenge. Her unmatched strength drives her to fight for the Shokan legacy and exact vengeance on those who wronged her.
Shinnok
Shinnok, once an Elder God, fell from divine grace to the Netherrealm after betrayal by fellow gods. He formed an alliance with Onaga to reclaim his lost supremacy.
Shinnok's schemes involved manipulating powerful figures and forming alliances with dark forces like Quan Chi. His ultimate goal is to overthrow all realms and restore himself as the supreme force of darkness.
Shujinko

Haunted by past mistakes, Shujinko seeks redemption by uncovering plots against Outworld. His quest is to defend the realm from emerging dangers and prove his worth.
Sindel
Sindel, Queen of Edenia, fights with the Forces of Light to restore her realm and end the cycle of destruction. Betrayed and imprisoned, she overcame immense trials.
Resurrected by dark forces and controlled by Shao Kahn, Sindel was freed by her daughter Kitana. Now, she stands firm against Armageddon, determined to reclaim Edenia and defeat the darkness.
Smoke
Smoke, once a loyal Lin Kuei cyber-ninja, struggled as a weapon against his friend Sub-Zero. Reprogrammed by Noob Saibot, he endured torment in the Netherrealm.
Now fighting with the Forces of Light, Smoke seeks redemption by targeting Noob Saibot, the embodiment of his torment and a symbol of the darkness that corrupted him.
Sonya
Sonyas focus shifted from battling criminal clans to defending Earthrealm from invasion during Shang Tsungs tournament. Post-Onaga, she faced the Tekunin threat, led by Sektor.

Tanya

Taven
Taven, son of the Elder God Argus, was awakened from a timeless slumber to defeat Blaze and prevent Armageddon. His quest reveals a world in chaos and betrayal.

Khameleon
