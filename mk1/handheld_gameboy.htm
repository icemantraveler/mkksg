Last updated: 05/12/19
Note: All commands are assuming your character is facing right.
If you are facing left, you must reverse the directional commands.
Backwards becoming Forwards and Vice Versa.
It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
P = Punch
K = Kick
BL = Block		 Game Boy:
P = B
K = A
BL = Start

A 2000 year old half-human dragon, Goro remains undefeated for the past 500 years. He won the title of Grand Champion by defeating Kung Lao, a Shaolin fighting monk. Special Moves:

Invisibility: (D+BL)
Stomp: (U+F)
Block: (P+K)
Play As Goro:
- Finish the entire game.
- Watch the ending credits roll by.
- When it says "THE END", press and HOLD (U+B+A+Select).
- Keep them held until you are asked to Enter your initials.
- Enter your initials, and press A.
- Wait until the HIGH SCORE SCREEN, and press Start.

You should be greeted by a message:

GORO LIVES...
... AS YOU !
