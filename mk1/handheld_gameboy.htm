It's recommended that your controls match the default setting.
Key:
U = Up/Jump
D = Down/Crouch
F = Forward/Toward
B = Back/Away
Universal:
P = Punch
K = Kick
BL = Block		 Game Boy:
P = B
K = A
BL = START
Fatality Distances Explained:
Sweep: A couple of steps away (close enough to land a sweep).
Half Screen: About one jump distance away from your opponent.
Anywhere: Distance doesn't matter, can be any range (outside sweep or further works best).

Goro (Sub-Boss)

Info:
Age: 2000+
Height: 8'2"
Weight: 850 lb
Hair: Black
Eyes: Red
Legal Status: Prince of Kuatan on Outworld
Known Relatives:
    King Gorbak (Father)
    Queen Mai (Mother)
    Durak (Brother - Deceased)
    Seven Wives
Birthplace: Kuatan, Fourth Astral Plane of Shokan, Realm of the Outworld
Occupation: Prince of Kuatan, Ruler Supreme of Shokan's Armies

Bio:
A 2000-year-old half-human dragon, Goro has been undefeated for the past 500 years. He won the title of Grand Champion by defeating Kung Lao, a Shaolin fighting monk. It was during this period that the tournament fell into Shang Tsung's hands and was corrupted. Goro possesses both massive strength and great agility. None who have fought him have reported any weaknesses - in fact, none who have opposed him have survived!

Special Moves:
Fireball: B, D, F, BL
Invisibility: (D+BL)
Stomp: (U+F)
Block: (P+K)

Play as Goro:
- Finish the entire game.
- Watch the ending credits roll by.
- When it says "THE END", press and HOLD (U+B+A+Select).
- Keep them held until you are asked to Enter your initials.
- Enter your initials, and press A.
- Wait until the HIGH SCORE SCREEN, and press START.

You should be greeted by a message:

GORO LIVES...
... AS YOU !
